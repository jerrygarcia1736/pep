Tips for Accurate Tracking:
  • Include quantities: "2 eggs" instead of just "eggs"
  • Be specific: "grilled chicken breast" vs "chicken"
  • Mention prep: "fried", "grilled", "baked", etc.
  • Multiple items: List all items in one entry separated by commas
  • Use common units: "1 cup", "2 oz", "100g", "1 medium apple"
Good Examples

"2 scrambled eggs with cheese, 1 slice whole wheat toast, 1 cup of black coffee"

"6 oz grilled chicken breast, 1 cup brown rice, 1 cup steamed broccoli"

"1 scoop whey protein powder, 1 banana, 1 cup almond milk"

"Large pepperoni pizza, 2 slices"
